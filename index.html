FAQ

Questions parents usually ask before they enroll.

FAQs reduce friction and make the site more useful. The old version had no enrollment details, which makes a service business look unfinished.

Are classes one-to-one or group based?

Most programs run in small live batches. Limited one-to-one support can be offered for specific academic needs.

Which curricula do you support?

The structure works well for CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, state boards, and competitive exam preparation with Physics-specific adjustments.

How do you handle doubts between classes?

Students can submit questions for scheduled doubt-clearing windows and mentor review during the week.

Do parents receive progress updates?

Yes. Feedback can include attendance, participation quality, test trends, and major concept gaps.
